Yield Minting Instructions

  • Approve BUSD Spend > Hire Minters > Minters Mint BUSD > Compound Daily > Withdraw > Profit!
  • Choose how much BUSD you want to spend and click "Approve Spend".
  • "Hire Minters" using BUSD.
  • Your minters will start minting, placing the BUSD they find into your briefcase.
  • You can compound your earnings using the "Hire More Minters" button. This uses the BUSD in your briefcase to increase your BUSD minting production rate.
  • Alternatively, you can withdraw the BUSD in your briefcase using the "Pocket Your BUSD" button and your minters will begin filling your briefcase again.

Minters will continue filling your briefcase with BUSD non-stop for up to 24 hours. After 24 hours your briefcase will become full and potentially begin overflowing until you've used the BUSD in your briefcase by either hiring more minters or pocketing it. You can hire more minters or pocket your BUSD at any time but it's recommended to do so at least once every 24 hours.

BUSD Yield Minting

BUSD Yield Minting is for users who want long term profits and sustainability. It is not for people who want instant profits that ultimately cause loss for other users.

Yield Minting is not to be confused with Yield Farming. Minters are not bought, they are hired and therefore cannot be sold. Once minters are hired they work for you forever, providing you BUSD earnings every day at a modest starting rate of 3%.

Our algorithm allows every user to profit handsomely, no matter their Minting Strategy. It will not allow any single user to have an unfair advantage over other users. We believe it is a truly beautiful thing.

Yield Minting Returns

BUSD Minter gives users 3% daily return, according to the current minting efficiency rate. The minting efficiency rate rises and falls as you and other users hire minters, compound earnings and pocket BUSD rewards.

Yield Minting Strategies

While the BUSD Minter algorithm makes sure that all users receive their fair share of rewards, it also favours its most supportive users through its compounding programme. Compounding can be done by clicking the "Hire More Minters" button.

Strategies are personal, you either "Hire More Minters" every day, "Pocket Your BUSD" every day, or you do a healthy combination of both. In our opinion the best strategy is to compound on daily basis. Once you're happy with your daily, weekly or monthly results, take profits as often as you see fit and simply continue compounding the rest.

Keep in mind that the 3% return is not constant, it will vary slightly based on a combination of factors, including your personal hiring, pocketing, compounding habits and the habits of the community as a whole. Your personal habits hold much more weight in regards to your overal personal returns. Also remember, just as that number can decrease it also increases based on a combination of those same habits.

After hiring any number of minters, you will begin to see returns very close to the advertised 3% daily, this number will rise and fall based on the factors explained above. We will not attempt to share any exact numbers because the maths behind the contract is complicated. You can see the results in the earnings you receive for yourself.

Contract Details

The contract is open source and visible on BscScan. It is rug free and immutable, meaning there are no functions that the dev can use to drain or change its operations.

The probability of the BUSD Minter contract balance becoming zero is highly unlikely due to our 3% maximum daily return and considering that many users are regularly compounding their daily returns for larger future profits.
